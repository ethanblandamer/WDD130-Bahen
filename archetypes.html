Who We Are Our Mission Find Your Phone

Simple User

Do you want a phone that works for you, rather than a phone you have to work for? If you find yourself rolling your eyes at complex settings and just want a device that is smart enough to handle the "small stuff"—like filtering spam or finding a photo from three years ago—without you having to dig through menus, then you’re a Simple User. Ask yourself: Do you value a clean, "clutter-free" screen over a million customization options? Is "seven years of peace of mind" more appealing to you than having the newest experimental gadget? If you’re nodding your head, you don't need a tech project in your pocket; you need a reliable, intelligent companion that stays out of your way and just works.

The Phone For You: Google Pixel 10 Pro

Photographer

Do you find yourself constantly stopping in your tracks because the light is hitting a building "just right," or because you spotted a texture that needs to be captured? If you’re the person in the friend group who is always designated as the "official photographer" because you actually care about framing and depth, then you’ve found your tribe. Ask yourself: Are you tired of photos that look over-processed and artificial? Do you wish you had a lens that could actually reach that distant detail without turning it into a blurry mess? If you answered "yes" to any of these, you aren't just a casual user—you’re a mobile artist. You deserve a device that treats a camera like a professional instrument rather than a social media afterthought.

The Phones For You: Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra, Google Pixel 10 Pro

Power User

Do you feel like your smartphone should be a high-performance workstation rather than just a communication device? If you’re the type of person who constantly juggles three different tasks at once, or if you feel "limited" when you can’t customize every single corner of your digital workspace, then you are a Power User. Ask yourself: Do you find yourself wishing you could plug your phone into a monitor and just start working? Does the idea of a built-in stylus or a desktop-grade processor get you excited about what you can actually accomplish on the go? If you answered "yes," you don't just want a phone; you want a tool that can keep up with your workflow and push the boundaries of what mobile hardware can do.

The Phones For You: OnePlus 13, Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra

Budget Watcher

Do you take pride in finding the "sweet spot" where high-end quality meets a sensible price tag? If you’re the type of person who loves the thrill of getting 90% of the flagship experience without paying the 100% "brand tax," then you are a Budget Watcher. Ask yourself: Do you find it hard to justify spending $1,200 on a glass rectangle when you know there’s a smarter way to spend that money? Does the idea of a phone that runs like a dream but costs half as much as the competition sound like a total win? If you’re nodding along, you aren't looking for "cheap"—you’re looking for a calculated, high-value investment that proves you don't have to overspend to stay ahead of the curve.

The Phone For You: Nothing Phone 3

Fashionist

Do you view your phone as the most important accessory you carry every single day? If you’re the person who chooses a device based on how it feels in your hand and how it complements your personal style rather than just what’s under the hood, then you are a Fashionist. Ask yourself: Are you tired of the same "gray and silver" slabs that everyone else is carrying? Do you want a phone that sparks a conversation the moment you put it down on the dinner table? If you find yourself drawn to unique textures, bold colors, or a design that feels like a piece of modern art, you don't just want a gadget—you want a statement piece that reflects who you are.

The Phones For You: Motorola Razr Ultra, Nothing Phone 3

Mobile Gamer

Do you look at your phone and see a portable console rather than just a communication device? If you’re the person who checks frame rates, cooling specs, and touch-sampling rates before you even look at the camera’s specs, then you are a Mobile Gamer. Ask yourself: Are you tired of your phone getting uncomfortably hot and slowing down right when the match gets intense? Do you wish you had physical buttons or triggers that gave you a real edge over everyone else using a flat glass screen? If this is the dream, you don’t just want a "fast" phone—you want a high-performance machine that treats your rank as seriously as you do.

The Phones For You: Redmagic 11 Pro, OnePlus 13
